Prologue

Our memory is like sands in our hand. We thought we were holding them firmly, but actually──There’s nothing we have on our hand. This is why this blog is here. It helps us to remember this precious memory. Especially for me, this precious memory almost has a range of 7 months. At least I have built this blog which might help you to learn my story.

Summary

This blog is writting for Maranatha. To memorize the time I came to study in Maranatha.

As you know English is my second language, so there would be a lot of difficulties for me to connect with you. So to avoid embrass, I chose to speak less. But it is very console to me that most of you would like to talk with me, especially reading this blog. Please read with patience, as I always speak with patience.

Experience

Beginning

I graduate from 10th grade in China to 10th grade here. Compare to other Chinese, my English would probably be the worst. So in the bignning I am kind of afraid to talk. Because once I talk with you guys, you might think me as one of the stupidest person you ever met. Although my English sucks. But at least I know staffs in math and science(I have already studied them in China, which is much harder and deeper). So it makes it much easier to me. But because the bad habit I have in Chinese high school, I forgot to turn homework in time in almost all the classes. That’s a little hash. But I got used to it faster than I thought.
